PROJECT OVERVIEW

Yup, it's a real hoverboard

The fourth instalment of the Amazing In Motion work for Lexus, Slide is the ultimate expression of our pursuit in exploring motion through technology and design. The amount of energy, talent, collaboration and triumph that surrounded this project was immense and was well awarded because of it. Happy days. Cool stuff. Here's an overview.
